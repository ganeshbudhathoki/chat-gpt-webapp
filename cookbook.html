Breakfast

  • Blueberry Pancakes

    Ingredients: flour, eggs, milk, blueberries, sugar, baking powder, salt

    Instructions: 1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. 2. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and milk together. 3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until just combined. 4. Fold in blueberries. 5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and scoop batter onto skillet. 6. Cook until bubbles form on surface, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes. 7. Serve with butter and syrup.

Lunch

  • Caesar Salad

    Ingredients: romaine lettuce, parmesan cheese, croutons, Caesar dressing, chicken

    Instructions: 1. Rinse and chop romaine lettuce. 2. Top with grated parmesan cheese, croutons and Caesar dressing. 3. Cook chicken and add it as a topping.
