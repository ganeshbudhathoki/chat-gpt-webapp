Breakfast

  • Blueberry Pancakes

    Ingredients: flour, eggs, milk, blueberries, sugar, baking powder, salt

    Instructions: 1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. 2. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and milk together. 3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until just combined. 4. Fold in blueberries. 5. Heat a skillet over medium heat
